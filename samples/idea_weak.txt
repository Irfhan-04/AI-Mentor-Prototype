We want to build an AI platform that uses blockchain and machine learning to revolutionize how students learn in the future. Our vision is to create a next-generation super-app that combines social media, productivity tools, marketplace features, and AI tutoring into one seamless ecosystem for the modern student. Students today are overwhelmed with too many separate apps, and we believe consolidating everything into a single platform powered by advanced AI will fundamentally change education forever. The app will have features like AI chatbots, gamified rewards using blockchain tokens, a social feed, a marketplace, virtual study rooms, and much more, all designed to keep students engaged and motivated. We are inspired by how big tech companies have built ecosystems that lock in users, and we want to do the same for the education space. We haven't decided on a specific first feature to launch or a specific group of students to target first, since we believe the platform's real value comes from having everything together rather than one narrow use case. We plan to figure out monetization once we have enough users, likely through a token economy. We are confident that if we build something ambitious enough, students will naturally gravitate toward it over time.
